BUSINESS PLAN: SecureShield Consulting

Executive Summary:
SecureShield provides cybersecurity assessment and implementation services for small-to-medium businesses lacking in-house security expertise.

Target Market: SMBs with 20-200 employees in healthcare, finance, and legal sectors facing regulatory compliance requirements.

Revenue Model: Retainer-based consulting ($5K-15K/month) plus project-based security audits and implementation services.

Competitive Advantage: Industry-specific compliance frameworks, 24/7 incident response, and fractional CISO services at competitive rates.
