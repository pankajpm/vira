BUSINESS PLAN: FreshBite Ghost Kitchen

Executive Summary:
FreshBite operates delivery-only kitchens serving healthy, chef-crafted meals through multiple virtual restaurant brands from a single location.

Target Market: Urban millennials and Gen Z consumers aged 25-40 seeking convenient, nutritious meal options.

Revenue Model: Direct sales through delivery apps and proprietary platform with 35% average margins.

Competitive Advantage: Lower overhead than traditional restaurants, data-driven menu optimization, 30-minute delivery radius.
