BUSINESS PLAN: Riverbend Craft Brewery

Executive Summary:
Riverbend is a local craft brewery producing small-batch beers with a waterfront taproom and beer garden. We focus on traditional styles with local ingredient sourcing and community events.

Target Market: Craft beer enthusiasts aged 25-55, tourists, and locals seeking social gathering spaces with quality beverages and food pairings.

Revenue Model: Taproom sales (50%), distribution to local bars/restaurants (35%), packaged retail (15%). Target margin 65% on taproom sales.

Competitive Advantage: Scenic location, seasonal brews, live music venue, partnerships with local food trucks.
