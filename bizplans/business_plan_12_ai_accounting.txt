BUSINESS PLAN: PrecisionBooks AI Accounting

Executive Summary:
PrecisionBooks deploys AI agents to automate bookkeeping, tax preparation, and financial analysis for small businesses. Agents continuously monitor transactions, flag anomalies, optimize tax strategies, and generate real-time insights.

Target Market: Small businesses with $500K-$10M revenue lacking in-house accounting expertise but needing sophisticated financial management.

Revenue Model: Flat monthly fees $500-2000 vs hourly billing. AI handles 85% of routine work, CPAs focus on strategy and complex issues.

Competitive Advantage: Real-time books, proactive tax optimization, fraction of traditional costs, instant financial dashboards.
