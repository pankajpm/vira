BUSINESS PLAN: ChargePoint Infrastructure Solutions

Executive Summary:
ChargePoint designs, installs, and operates EV charging networks for commercial properties, municipalities, and fleet operators, generating recurring revenue from charging fees and network management.

Target Market: Shopping centers, apartment complexes, office parks, municipal parking, logistics companies with electric fleets. Properties with 100+ parking spaces in urban/suburban markets.

Total Addressable Market: $42B global EV charging infrastructure market by 2030, US market $18B. Our serviceable market: $850M across major metro areas.

Revenue Projections:
Year 1: $3.2M (200 chargers installed)
Year 2: $7.8M (550 chargers)
Year 3: $16M (1,200 chargers)
Year 4: $28M (2,000 chargers)
Year 5: $48M (3,200 chargers)

Profit Projections (EBITDA):
Year 1: -$400K (infrastructure investment)
Year 2: $1.2M (15% margin)
Year 3: $3.4M (21% margin)
Year 4: $7M (25% margin)
Year 5: $13.4M (28% margin)

Competitive Advantage: Turnkey solutions, shared revenue model requiring no upfront capital from property owners, AI-optimized pricing, preventive maintenance, grid integration expertise.
