BUSINESS PLAN: HomeHub Services Network

Executive Summary:
HomeHub coordinates plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and handyman services using AI agents that triage service requests, dispatch technicians, provide instant quotes, manage inventory, and handle customer communications for a network of independent contractors.

Target Market: Homeowners and property managers needing reliable, fast home repair services. Licensed contractors wanting more jobs with less admin work.

Revenue Model: 18% commission on jobs. AI handles customer intake, scheduling, quoting, invoicing, and follow-up. Contractors get 30% more billable hours.

Competitive Advantage: Instant quotes, smart routing, predictive parts ordering, automated warranty tracking, quality assurance.
