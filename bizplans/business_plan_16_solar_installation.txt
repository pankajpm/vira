BUSINESS PLAN: SunPeak Solar Installations

Executive Summary:
SunPeak provides residential and commercial solar panel installation with battery storage systems, serving the growing renewable energy market.

Target Market: Homeowners with $80K+ income in sunny regions, small businesses with high energy costs (retail, warehouses, agriculture). Secondary market: new construction developers.

Total Addressable Market: $28B US residential solar market, growing 15% annually. Our serviceable market: $450M across 3-state region.
