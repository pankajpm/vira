BUSINESS PLAN: EcoRetro Building Solutions

Executive Summary:
EcoRetro specializes in commercial building retrofits for energy efficiency, HVAC upgrades, LED lighting, insulation, and smart building controls. Services offered on performance contract basis where savings fund improvements.

Target Market: Older commercial buildings (20+ years), manufacturing facilities, warehouses, schools, and government buildings with $50K+ annual energy costs seeking 30-50% energy reduction.

Total Addressable Market: $279B US commercial building retrofit market. Serviceable market: $3.8B across Northeast and Mid-Atlantic regions with aging building stock.

Revenue Projections:
Year 1: $5.5M (22 projects)
Year 2: $10.2M (38 projects)
Year 3: $18M (62 projects)
Year 4: $29M (92 projects)
Year 5: $44M (130 projects)

Profit Projections (EBITDA):
Year 1: $650K (12% margin)
Year 2: $1.5M (15% margin)
Year 3: $3.1M (17% margin)
Year 4: $5.5M (19% margin)
Year 5: $9.2M (21% margin)

Competitive Advantage: Performance guarantees, no upfront cost to clients, certified energy audits, proprietary ROI modeling, access to utility rebates and federal tax credits, ongoing monitoring services.
