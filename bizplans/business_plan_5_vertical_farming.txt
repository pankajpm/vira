BUSINESS PLAN: VerticalHarvest Urban Farms

Executive Summary:
VerticalHarvest builds and operates hydroponic vertical farms in urban warehouses, producing fresh leafy greens and herbs year-round for local restaurants and grocery stores.

Target Market: Farm-to-table restaurants, organic grocery chains, and meal kit services within 50-mile delivery radius.

Revenue Model: Direct B2B sales with contracts guaranteeing 90% less water usage and 95% reduction in food miles versus traditional farming.

Competitive Advantage: Controlled environment eliminates pesticides, enables 365-day growing, premium pricing.
