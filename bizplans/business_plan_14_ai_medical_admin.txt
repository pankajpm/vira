BUSINESS PLAN: CareFlow Medical Practice Services

Executive Summary:
CareFlow provides turnkey practice management where AI agents handle patient scheduling, insurance verification, prior authorizations, billing, collections, and patient communication, allowing physicians to focus entirely on care delivery.

Target Market: Independent primary care and specialty practices with 2-10 physicians drowning in administrative burden and struggling with reimbursement.

Revenue Model: 8% of collections vs 15% for traditional billing services. AI agents process claims 10x faster with 95% first-pass acceptance rate.

Competitive Advantage: Automated prior auth appeals, intelligent scheduling optimization, proactive denial management, patient engagement.
