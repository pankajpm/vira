BUSINESS PLAN: Artisan Grounds Coffee Roastery

Executive Summary:
Artisan Grounds is a specialty coffee roastery and cafe featuring single-origin beans roasted on-site. We offer premium coffee, pastries, and a community gathering space in downtown.

Target Market: Coffee enthusiasts aged 25-55, remote workers, and local residents seeking quality coffee experiences.

Revenue Model: Retail cafe sales (60%), wholesale to local restaurants (25%), online bean sales (15%). Average ticket $8-12.

Competitive Advantage: Direct trade relationships with farmers, educational tastings, unique roasting profiles.
