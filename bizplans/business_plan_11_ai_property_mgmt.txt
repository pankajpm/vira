BUSINESS PLAN: SmartManage Property Services

Executive Summary:
SmartManage revolutionizes property management using AI agents that autonomously handle tenant communications, maintenance coordination, rent collection, and lease renewals for multi-family buildings.

Target Market: Property owners with 50-500 units seeking to reduce management overhead while improving tenant satisfaction scores.

Revenue Model: 4% of gross rent (vs industry 8-10%) enabled by AI efficiency. Agents handle 90% of routine tasks including emergency triage and vendor scheduling.

Competitive Advantage: 24/7 tenant response, predictive maintenance, automated compliance tracking, 60% cost reduction vs traditional management.
