BUSINESS PLAN: PawsPerfect Mobile Grooming

Executive Summary:
PawsPerfect brings professional pet grooming services directly to customers' homes using fully-equipped grooming vans. We eliminate pet stress and owner inconvenience with doorstep service.

Target Market: Pet owners in suburban neighborhoods earning $75K+, particularly elderly owners and busy professionals with limited mobility or time.

Revenue Model: Per-service pricing: $65-150 based on pet size and services. Subscription packages offer 10% discounts for regular monthly appointments.

Competitive Advantage: Convenience, reduced pet anxiety, premium organic products, flexible scheduling.
