BUSINESS PLAN: CoreBuild Data Center Construction

Executive Summary:
CoreBuild is a specialized general contractor focused exclusively on data center construction and critical infrastructure, serving hyperscalers, colocation providers, and enterprise clients requiring mission-critical facilities with 99.999% uptime requirements.

Target Market: Cloud providers, colocation operators, financial services, healthcare systems, AI/ML companies building GPU clusters. Projects range $20M-$200M primarily in tier-3 and tier-4 markets with power availability.
