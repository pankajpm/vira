BUSINESS PLAN: ModBuild Construction Systems

Executive Summary:
ModBuild manufactures and installs modular buildings for commercial, educational, and healthcare sectors using factory-built components, reducing construction time by 50% and costs by 30%.

Target Market: School districts needing rapid classroom expansion, healthcare systems building clinics, companies requiring temporary offices, affordable housing developers. Focus on projects $500K-$5M.

Total Addressable Market: $157B North American modular construction market, growing 6.8% annually. Our serviceable market: $2.1B in Western region.

Revenue Projections:
Year 1: $8M (16 projects)
Year 2: $15M (28 projects)
Year 3: $26M (45 projects)
Year 4: $42M (68 projects)
Year 5: $65M (98 projects)

Profit Projections (EBITDA):
Year 1: $800K (10% margin)
Year 2: $2.1M (14% margin)
Year 3: $4.2M (16% margin)
Year 4: $7.6M (18% margin)
Year 5: $13M (20% margin)

Competitive Advantage: 60-day delivery vs 6-month traditional builds, controlled factory quality, weather-independent production, financing options, sustainable materials.
