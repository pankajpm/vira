BUSINESS PLAN: CoreStrength Boutique Fitness

Executive Summary:
CoreStrength is a premium boutique fitness studio offering small-group training classes (max 12 people) in HIIT, yoga, pilates, and strength training with personalized attention from certified instructors.

Target Market: Health-conscious professionals aged 28-50 willing to pay premium prices for personalized fitness experiences.

Revenue Model: Class packages ($200-800/month), personal training ($80-120/session), retail activewear and supplements.

Competitive Advantage: Intimate class sizes, results tracking system, luxury amenities including showers and towel service.
