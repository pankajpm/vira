BUSINESS PLAN: EcoThreads

Executive Summary:
EcoThreads is an online marketplace connecting consumers with verified sustainable fashion brands. We curate eco-friendly clothing and accessories while providing transparency about environmental impact.

Target Market: Environmentally conscious consumers aged 22-45, primarily women, with household income $60K+.

Revenue Model: 20% commission on sales plus premium brand partnerships and subscription boxes at $49/month.

Competitive Advantage: Rigorous sustainability certification process and carbon-neutral shipping program.
