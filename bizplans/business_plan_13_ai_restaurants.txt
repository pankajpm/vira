BUSINESS PLAN: OptiDine Restaurant Group

Executive Summary:
OptiDine operates casual dining restaurants where AI agents dynamically manage inventory ordering, staff scheduling, menu pricing, supplier negotiations, and waste reduction based on real-time demand patterns and ingredient costs.

Target Market: Urban and suburban locations serving 500-1000 covers weekly, targeting families and professionals seeking quality casual dining.

Revenue Model: Food sales with 28% food cost (vs industry 32%) and 22% labor cost (vs 30%) through AI optimization. 5-location pilot expanding to 25 locations.

Competitive Advantage: AI reduces waste by 40%, optimizes pricing hourly, predicts demand accurately, negotiates supplier contracts.
