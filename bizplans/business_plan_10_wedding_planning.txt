BUSINESS PLAN: Ever After Wedding Planning

Executive Summary:
Ever After provides full-service wedding planning and day-of coordination, managing all aspects from venue selection to vendor coordination, ensuring stress-free celebrations for couples.

Target Market: Engaged couples aged 25-40 with wedding budgets of $30K-100K seeking professional expertise and peace of mind during planning.

Revenue Model: Tiered packages from $3K (day-of coordination) to $15K (full planning). Average 25-30 weddings annually. Commission relationships with preferred vendors.

Competitive Advantage: Established vendor network, crisis management expertise, destination wedding specialization.
